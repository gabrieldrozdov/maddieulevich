BIOGRAPHY

A passion for theater and arts performance and administration, with a particular love for musical theater and development.

Maddie is a professionally trained actor and singer based in New York City with experience in both classical and contemporary theater and musical theater. She earned her Bachelors degree in Theater with an acting concentration from Wesleyan University in 2019. Prior to transferring to Wesleyan, Maddie also spent a year in the undergraduate acting program at Emerson college. In addition to theater, Maddie is a trained musician, with nearly a decade of coursework in music theory, analysis, composition and orchestration. She has music directed two full-length theater productions and numerous smaller projects.
